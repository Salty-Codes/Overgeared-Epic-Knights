ORIGINAL MODS
-------------
This compatibility mod builds upon the work of:

Epic Knights: Shields, Armor and Weapons
  - Modrinth: https://modrinth.com/mod/epic-knights-shields-armor-and-weapons
  - Medieval weapons and armor system

Overgeared
  - Modrinth: https://modrinth.com/mod/overgeared
  - Material-based forging and crafting system
